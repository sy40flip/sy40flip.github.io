"the corridor"   exhibition

"THE CORRIDOR" project was self-funded, and shown as a pilot project in the exhibition. It was held on July 8-10, 2016 at the Dreamlike Bubble Gallery. We aim to make it as a first attempt to find out the hidden technical problems, and discover a better way to deliver artist's ideas in this interactive form.

the exhibition shows "the corridor", an immersive environment that allows visitors to dwell into. the visitors' footstep helps unfold the screen content.

Only one person is allowed inside "the corridor" each time. one can walk back and forth inside "the corridor", as the perception may change when faced with the same scenario the second time.

outside "the corridor", the concepts behind "the corridor" are presented to the visitors. One can take time to read and reflect about the experience.

next to "the corridor" experience, there is a lounge area, where you can sit down; kick off your shoes, and do some quiz that we prepared for you.

pick a color sticker that represents you, and share your reflection on "the corridor" experience.

or you can take a more active approach, write down your comments about the experience. it may arouse some of your old memories and need time to recall those moments and feelings.

"the corridor" exhibition was held in "dream like bubble", a place full of vitality and diversity. Its welcoming environment seem to await those who has something to say, and expect someone to stir up new ideas through exchanging different views.

each visitor was expected to walk into "the corridor" with bare foot, as the corridor ground was laid with acoustic foam. During sharing, visitors gave very positive feedback on this tactile feeling, while walking down corridor and experiencing the story content. We also discussed how such setting relates to the concept of the art work.

behind the scene, the interactivity of the artwork is powered by a circuit board which detects gestures and movement of the visitors. The senstivity of this sensor is crucial in whether the technology helps or kills the delivery of screen content. Visitors do give back valuable comments on the result.

after all the technical problems are solved, our goal is that, the artwork speaks a kind of language that crosses cultural boundary, and like music, that goes beyond words and reasons.
