Pete's Works

>>> artwork | experiment

  • The Corridor ——— Experiment on the relationship between interactivity and circular narrative structure by developing a physical set of corridor, in which the screen content is unfolded through visitors footsteps.
  • Soblind Express ——— Experiment on the time factor in a narrative experience in which time has no power to advance the plot. The narrative experience will only be revealed to viewers when they participate in it.
  • TV Switch ——— Experiment on the unilinear timeline by building a webpage with left and right interactive zones, which drive the visual backward and forward.
  • Home Sweet Home ——— Experiment on building an audio-dominant narrative sequence on web platform
  • Easter Talk ——— Experiment on the relationship between interactivity and character animation
  • The Frame ——— Experiment on the relationship between interactivity and the frame of the screen
  • Remake Everyday ——— Experiment on recreating the downtime of everyday life solely through the power of video editing
  • 699 ——— Experiment on deconstructing the integrated synthesis of different components, type, image and audio in a sequence. Each component is separated as individual channel which carries message of its own
  • Orange ——— Experiment on the relationship between color and the emotional experience of a character
